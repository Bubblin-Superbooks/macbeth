1. Walke. Enter Banquo and Fleans, with a Torch.
  2. A Light, a Light
  3. 'Tis hee
  4. Stand too't

Ban. It will be Rayne to Night

  1. Let it come downe

Ban. O, Trecherie! Flye good Fleans, flye, flye, flye, Thou may'st reuenge. O Slaue!

  1. Who did strike out the Light?
  2. Was't not the way?
  3. There's but one downe: the Sonne is fled
  4. We haue lost Best halfe of our Affaire
  5. Well, let's away, and say how much is done.

Exeunt.

Scaena Quarta.
